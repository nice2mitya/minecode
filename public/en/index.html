❓ Frequently asked questions

Do you need programming experience?
No. The course is designed to start from scratch. The main thing is curiosity and a willingness to think.
How much time do you need per week?
Optimally 2-3 short sessions of 25-40 minutes. You can go at your own pace.
Is this about Python or about Minecraft?
Both: Minecraft is a clear metaphor, Python is a language for practice and systems thinking.
