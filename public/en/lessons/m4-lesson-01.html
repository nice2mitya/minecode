What is AI (without magic)

Let’s look at AI as a pattern search tool, not a “magic brain.”

🎣 Hook

AI seems like magic until you break it down into steps: data, training, testing, applying.

🧠 Concept

Terms: list, dictionary, loop, function - we write in English, we explain in Russian.

The AI model learns to find patterns in the data.

Important to remember:
- the model does not “understand the world” like a person;
- it predicts the most likely answer;
- quality depends on data and verification.
