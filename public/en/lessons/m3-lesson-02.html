Binary search

We search quickly in the sorted data, dividing the range in half.

🎣 Hook

You are looking for a book in an alphabetical catalogue: you open the middle, understand which half to go to, repeat.

🧠 Concept

Terms: list, dictionary, loop, function - we write in English, we explain in Russian.

Binary search only works on sorted data.
Steps:
1) take the middle;
2) compare with the target;
3) discard half the range.

Complexity: O(log n).
