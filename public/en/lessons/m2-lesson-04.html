Observer Pattern

Event-driven thinking: reacting to changes rather than endlessly questioning them.

🎣 Hook

The Observer block in Minecraft catches the change nearby and immediately gives a signal. It doesn't "check every millisecond."

🧠 Concept

Terms: list, dictionary, loop, function - we write in English, we explain in Russian.

The Observer pattern connects the event source and subscribers.

Source: “the state of the door has changed.”
Subscribers: lamp, siren, logger.

In code, these are often callback functions:
- subscribe to the event;
- receive a notification;
- perform an action.
