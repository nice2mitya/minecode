Sorting - order in the chest

We organize the data for easy searching and processing.

🎣 Hook

When the resources in the chest are chaotic, you are wasting time. Sorting saves effort and stress.

🧠 Concept

Terms: list, dictionary, loop, function - we write in English, we explain in Russian.

Sorting is a rearrangement of elements according to a rule (ascending, descending, priority).

At the start, it is useful to understand bubble sort: neighboring elements are swapped until everything is in order.

It is important not only “how”, but also “why”: after sorting, many operations are accelerated.
