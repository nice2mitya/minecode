Optimization - why the server lags

We reduce time/memory and remove bottlenecks.

🎣 Hook

When a server lags, the problem is not in “magic”, but in specific hot spots: extra cycles, heavy operations, extra entities.

🧠 Concept

Terms: list, dictionary, loop, function - we write in English, we explain in Russian.

Optimization starts with measurement.

Approach:
1) measure (where it’s slow);
2) looking for a bottleneck;
3) change the algorithm/structure;
4) measure again.

Without measurements, it’s easy to “optimize” the wrong thing.
